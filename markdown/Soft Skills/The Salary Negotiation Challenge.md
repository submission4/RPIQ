# Scenario
Jane has been an exemplary employee, consistently exceeding expectations. She believes it's time for a salary raise to reflect her dedication and accomplishments. Mr. Johnson, her manager, acknowledges her contributions but needs to consider budget constraints. Navigate the negotiation process, highlight achievements, and find a compromise that satisfies both parties.
